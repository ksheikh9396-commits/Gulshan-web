Welcome

A Taste of Tradition

Founded in 1952, Gulshan Restaurant is a long-standing favorite in the city's dining scene, known for its flavorful meals and welcoming, relaxed atmosphere. Strategically located in the heart of the city, it serves a diverse selection of Kenyan, Swahili, and international dishes, consistently praised for generous portions, affordable prices, and comforting, satisfying meals.

Over generations, Gulshan has become a place of tradition, with many patrons first visiting as students and now returning with their families. Celebrated for its reliability and familiar flavors, Gulshan continues to provide a timeless, memorable dining experience for both locals and visitors alike. We invite you to experience dining that nourishes both body and soul.
